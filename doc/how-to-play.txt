A light sim game created to help me learn web development.
You must create ten sound design memes to escape your parents' basement (victory).
You must pay $30 in bills every week or lose your MemeNet account (defeat).

Every day you can take one of four actions:
Attempt Meme, Train Memery, Work, Attempt Education.

Attempt Meme: Has a 10% chance to create a meme.
Train Memery: Increases the success chance of Attempt Meme by 2%.
Work: Earn $8.
Attempt Education: 25% chance to increase salary from working by $2.
